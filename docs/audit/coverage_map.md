- **Sources reviewed:** custom DocType models under `apps/apps/ferum_custom/ferum_custom/ferum_custom/doctype`, fixtures in `apps/apps/ferum_custom/ferum_custom/fixtures`, and server logic (notably service, invoice, attachment, and integration handlers).
- **Legend:** P0 = must fix to go live, P1 = important but can follow shortly, P2 = nice to have. Effort is estimated qualitatively (S/M/L).

## Fixture landscape

- **Workflows:** see `apps/apps/ferum_custom/ferum_custom/fixtures/workflow.json` (Service Request, Service Report, Service Project, Invoice).
- **Roles & Role Profiles:** bundled in `role.json`, `role_profile.json`, `module_profile.json`.
- **Notifications & Assignment Rules:** defined in `notification.json`, `assignment_rule.json`, wired to custom doctypes and standard `Issue`.
- **Dashboard Charts & Workspaces:** curated through fixtures plus `site_ops.apply_ru_workspaces`.

## Key gaps to resolve before go-live

1. **Complete migrations to standard DocTypes (Requests, Reports, Projects, Invoices).** Patches exist but need staging data, UAT, and communication plan.
2. **Introduce integration guardrails (feature flags, healthchecks, error telemetry).** Current implementations rely on silent logs; risk of undetected backlog.
3. **Reduce Python-based permission logic.** Prefer declarative permissions and document exceptions explicitly.
